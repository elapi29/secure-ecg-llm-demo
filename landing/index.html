Problem

Raw metadata handoffs create risk

In healthtech and clinical AI workflows, sensitive metadata often moves into downstream systems with too little control, too little visibility, and too little protection.

Weak control

Metadata can be passed between systems as a raw handoff, without enough protection before reaching tools, agents, or AI pipelines.

Limited traceability

Teams need more confidence in what was sent, what was verified, and what was recovered before data moves downstream.

Security concerns

Clinical and biosignal workflows increasingly need security-aware handling from the first step, not only at the very end.

Solution

A secure ingestion layer for clinical metadata

This MVP demonstrates a simple but important idea: protect metadata before downstream processing, then verify and recover it in a controlled backend workflow.

1

Sign in browser

Establish authenticity before metadata leaves the client side.

2

3

Verify in backend

4

Recover structured payload

Return usable metadata for tools, applications, or AI pipelines.
